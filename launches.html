Mars 2020 Mission

LAUNCH LOCATION

Perseverance will launch from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station, Florida.

Because off planetary alignment, the only time to launch a large payload to the Red Planet occurs just three weeks out of every 26 months. If Perseverance doesn´t make it off the launch pad before the end of this launch opportunity (July 17- August 5), the project will have to wait until September 2022 to try again.

No matter what time Perseverance launches it will land at Mars'Jezero Crater on February 18, 2021 and explore the landscape for at least one Mars year (nearly two Earth years).

ARTEMIS MOON MISSION

ARE YOU THE FIRST WOMAN ON THE MOON?

By 2024 the first woman and next man will land on the surface of the Moon.

The Artemis 2 mission plan is to send four astronauts (two from NASA and one from Japan´s space agency) in the first crewed Orion capsule into a lunar flyby for a maximum of 21 days. The mission profile is a multitranslunar injection (MTLI), or multiple departure burns, and includes a free return trajectory from the Moon. Basically, the spacecraft will orbit Earth twice while periodically firing its engines to build up enough velocity to push it toward the Moon before looping back to Earth.
